The Single-Window Automation Ecosystem

Veltonics is a premier engineering firm dedicated to optimizing India's manufacturing sector. We recognize that modern factories require more than just a parts supplier—they require a dedicated technology partner. We bridge the gap between global hardware manufacturers and local production floors by offering a complete, end-to-end automation ecosystem.

1. Component Distribution

Deep local inventory of premium sensors, vision systems, PLCs, and safety devices. We offer unbiased, brand-agnostic hardware selection based on your exact application needs.

2. Turnkey Integration & SPMs

From concept to commissioning. We design and build custom Special Purpose Machines (SPMs) and robotic cells tailored perfectly to your production bottlenecks.

3. Control Panel Engineering

In-house design and assembly of robust electrical control panels, including advanced PLC programming and seamless SCADA/HMI architecture.

4. Annual Maintenance (AMC)

Protecting your investment. We offer comprehensive AMCs for automation lines, providing preventive maintenance and emergency troubleshooting to guarantee uptime.

Vision

To become India's most trusted, technically proficient partner for industrial automation, enabling local manufacturers to achieve zero-defect production through advanced technology and reliable engineering support.

Mission

To reduce machine downtime and eliminate production bottlenecks by acting as a single, accountable source for hardware procurement, custom machine building (SPM), and lifecycle maintenance (AMC).
